Welcome and Important Information

Welcome all to the PTA's Annual Summer Fair 2026.

Please read the below schedule of activities, how to claim a prize of sweets and icecream and important information about the event, in advance to avoid any disappointment. Certain activities are only available at certain times, please see schedule to avoid disappointment. How to claim prizes? Start at reception, collect an activity sheet. Win bag of sweets if you complete the treasure hunt or 4 starred activities. See maps for starred activities. You can't win more than 1 bag of sweets. Wanna win more, complete the treasure hunt and 4 starred activities and you win an ice cream. Collect winnings at reception. Wanna join Water gun fight? You have to bring empty water guns, labelled with your name - these will be filled up by PTA while you play. . Water guns and shields are to be dropped to reception on arrival. IMPORTANT: water guns are not to used during the event until water gun fight announced @4:45 pm. We may also advise to bring rain coats for children for water gun fights.
