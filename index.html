🎉 Welcome & Important Information

Welcome to the PTA’s Annual Summer Fair 2026! A free, inclusive and fun-filled afternoon for everyone 🌞

Please check the maps, activity schedule and important information below before getting started — some activities are only available at certain times so please read, to avoid any disappointment. Tickets???? This is a FREE event! Please book your ticket so we can gauge numbers (to arrange prizes in advance for example). Link to ticket system will be sent to you via Aladdin/email shortly.

🍭 How to Win Treats

Start at Reception and collect your activity sheet.

  • Complete the Treasure Hunt OR any 4 starred activities to win a bag of sweets 🍬
  • There are 9 starred activities hidden around the fair — check the maps!
  • Only 1 bag of sweets per child

Want to win even more? 👀

Complete BOTH the Treasure Hunt AND 4 starred activities to win an ice cream 🍦

Collect all prizes from Reception. Good luck! ☘️

💦 Water Gun Fight Rules

  • Bring an empty water gun (and shields if you have them!)
  • Please put your name/label on your items
  • Leave water guns at Reception when you arrive
  • The PTA will fill them while you enjoy the fair
  • Raincoats or splash-proof clothes are optional and at your parent's discretion 😄

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Water guns must not be used during the fair before the official Water Gun Fight begins at 4:45 P.M.

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Parents, we have specifically arranged for the Water Gun Fight to happen at the end of the event, so families may feel free to leave a little early to avoid it.

🌿 Need a Quiet Spot?

We’ve got you covered 💚

There is a dedicated Sensory Area and Quiet Area available during the fair. Please help us keep noise to a minimum in these spaces.

If you have them, please bring ear defenders/headphones, as some other areas of the fair may be louder.

If the weather is poor, the Sensory Area may move indoors and space may be limited to children with sensory needs — thank you for understanding.

🌧️ Irish Summer… What If It Rains?

We have a Plan B! 😄

As many activities as possible will move indoors if the weather turns wet.

If needed, updated Plan B maps will be uploaded closer to the event 🤞
